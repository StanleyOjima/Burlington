The Engineer

Nkem is an Einstein Visa (EB-1A) holder and technical executive with over 15 years building regulated, multi-market platforms around identity, payments, compliance, and trust systems across Microsoft, Hewlett Packard, and Dimension Data.

At Microsoft, he served as Engineering Innovation Lead across 26 countries, shaping product strategy, building platform infrastructure, and driving execution end-to-end. That experience building systems at continental scale is what he now brings to Burlington Consult's operations and technology infrastructure.

Nkem is a TEDx speaker on Africa's innovation ecosystem and the Fourth Industrial Revolution. He holds an MBA from the University of Manchester Business School and is PMP certified. He is also the founder of Nesi, a technology venture focused on African markets.

As Co-founder of Burlington Consult, Nkem oversees the firm's technology architecture, client systems, and operational infrastructure across three continents.
