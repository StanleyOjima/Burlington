What we look for

Three things that matter more than your CV.

We care about what you can do, not where you've been. If you can think clearly, move fast, and take ownership — we want to talk.

You don't wait for instructions. You see a problem, figure out a solution, and ship it. Perfection is the enemy of done — and done is what we're building on.
