Founder & Lead Adviser
CO
Earl Chukwuebuka "Chris" Ogbodo
Founder & Lead Adviser

Chris founded Burlington Consult to do for others what he did for himself — navigate the U.S. immigration and admissions system from outside it, and win.

He holds an LL.M. from Harvard Law School in Technology Law and AI Policy. He is a self-petitioned Einstein Visa (EB-1A) holder, approved on first submission. He was Africa's first Google Policy Fellow.

He has interviewed at final rounds at Harvard, Stanford, Wharton, OpenAI, Columbia, SpaceX, and the Tony Blair Institute — and built a career across Meta, Zipline, Luminate, Biglaw, PwC, Google, and Harvard Law from outside and inside the U.S. system.

He has secured over $500,000 in graduate scholarships and fellowships from Harvard, Columbia, Michigan Ross, Georgetown, and USC. Every engagement at Burlington Consult is informed by that direct, lived experience.
